places must visit

Machu Pichu, Peru

I visited here in 2022 spring break.

A large tourist attraction. It is one of the world wonders. This is a site made by the Incas in the 1500s to keep themeselves away from the invasive Spandiards. This historic site was never found by the spaniards, but a group of explorers in the early 1900s. To see the full view of this lost city, you must hike for 7 hours up the Machu Pichu Mountain which is at 9,000 feet in the air. Although it is 9,000 feet in the air, the hike is only 2,000 feet upwards.



London, England

I visited London in 2017.

This city is located in England, and is also the capital. Here there are many things to do. One thing is visiting the Ben Tower, which is a gigantic clock tower. Another place that you could visit is the Buckingham Palace. This is the palace in which the Queen of England along with the royal family lives. There are also many more tourist attractions that you could visit, but these were some of the main ones. London is a great city to visit, and you should go there and check it out!



Big Island, USA

I visited Big Island in 2019.

The Big Island is part of a series of islands that make up the state of Hawaii. It is a great tourist attraction mainly because of the volcanoes that are there. The main volcano is called Kīlauea and is not active at the moment. It had errupted recently in September of 2021. When I visited, I walked on the surface of the volcano and took a test to become a junior ranger there. The beaches there are great and very relaxing. The visit there was great, and there were so many great activities to do. It is a nice place to visit and you will probably never get bored.

  • LEGO plans to find replacement material for bricks by 2030

    In an effort to be more environmentally responsible, LEGO have said they aim to stop manufacturing bricks made of plastic resin and are considering other options, which could include hemp. While LEGO want to replace the harmful plastic used in their products, they also require the new material to meet the high standards that allow the blocks to work as they always have, especially with the effective “clicking” together is a key aspect of the iconic coloured block.

  • Amazon deforestation and number of fires show summer of 2019 not a 'normal' year

    The perceived scale of the Amazon blazes received global attention this summer. However, international concerns raised at the time were countered by the Brazilian Government, which claimed the fire situation in August was 'normal' and 'below the historical average'.An international team of scientists writing in the journal Global Change Biology say the number of active fires in August was actually three times higher than in 2018 and the highest number since 2010.
